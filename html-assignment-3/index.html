The Importance of Sleep

Written by Maaz Ahmad

Getting enough sleep is critically important for your long-term health. It serves as the foundation for your well-being, helping your body recover, repair, and stay mentally focused throughout the day.

A common phase of the sleep cycle is REM sleep, which is essential for memory consolidation and mood regulation.

Medical opinions on sleep duration have evolved over time. Experts now say we need at least 6 8 hours of sleep every night to function at our peak.

As the saying goes, A good laugh and a long sleep are the best cures in the doctor's book.

A leading specialist once noted:

Sleep is not a luxury. It is a necessity for the human body and mind. Without it, the cognitive and physical systems we rely on begin to degrade.
